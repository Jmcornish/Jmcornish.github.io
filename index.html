Problem Statement: What the Dog doin?

The decision of where you take your pets is an ever-evolving issue, it is a relevant issue to all pet owners. The issue of a truly safe play area for the dogs is complicated, because most dog parks do not employ safety measures that protects against dog-on-dog violence. This issue is even more important for puppies who may not have the social experience for them to socialize between dogs. This is a very serious issue because they are most likely to either get injured or injure other dogs or even themselves.
